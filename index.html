Who We Are

For Social Welfare,
By the Community

A non-profit organization dedicated to promoting social welfare by addressing poverty, inequality, and access to essential services.

Our trust works with communities, government agencies, and other NGOs to implement sustainable programs that improve the quality of life for disadvantaged groups across Tamil Nadu.

Founded under the Indian Trust Act 1882 for public social, educational and charitable purposes — we are committed to reducing hunger, spreading education, and building healthier communities.

What We Do

Our Core Programs

Every initiative is designed to create lasting impact — from the plate to the classroom, the clinic to the community.

🍱

Food Rescue & Distribution

Excess untouched food from marriage halls and hotels is inspected, safely packaged, and delivered to underserved communities — reducing waste and hunger simultaneously.

🏥

Healthcare Initiatives

Free medical camps, health awareness workshops, and access to affordable healthcare for low-income families across Chennai.

🎒

Education Programs

Scholarships, school supplies, Right to Education camps, and skill-based training for children and youth to secure better futures.

🌊

Disaster Relief

Assisting communities affected by natural disasters with immediate relief packages and long-term rehabilitation support.

🎁

Joy of Giving

Distributing bedsheets, slippers, juice, sweets, new clothes, and daily necessities — bringing comfort and happiness to those in need.

💼

Livelihood Support

Empowering individuals with job training, microfinance, and prosthetic limb donations for those with disabilities.

🛕

Temple Renovation

Restoring the Sri Patabhishega Ramar Temple. Support our religious trust initiative. Visit Temple Site →

How It Works

Food Safety is Our Priority

Every meal we rescue follows strict food safety standards before it reaches the hungry.

1

Get in Touch

Donors with surplus food from marriage halls, outdoor events, or hotels contact us at 9094242932.

2

Quality Check

Excess food is thoroughly inspected based on our Food Safety and Standard Guidelines for freshness and safety.

3

Locate Hunger Spots

We identify the nearest most underserved areas where food distribution will have the highest impact.

4

Deliver with Love

Safe, nutritious food is distributed to people in need — with care, dignity, and warmth.
